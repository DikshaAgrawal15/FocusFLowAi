FocusFlow AI 🚀
Smart AI Productivity Companion

FocusFlow AI is an intelligent productivity management system that helps users prioritize tasks, monitor deadlines, analyze productivity, and improve focus using AI-powered recommendations.

Problem Statement

Many students and professionals miss deadlines because existing to-do applications only store tasks but do not intelligently analyze priorities or provide proactive recommendations.

FocusFlow AI solves this problem using AI-driven task prioritization, risk prediction, productivity analytics, and gamification.
